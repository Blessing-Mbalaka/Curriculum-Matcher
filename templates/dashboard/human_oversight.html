Human-in-the-loop learning
AI suggests; people decide what becomes knowledge.

This page is the review layer between raw extraction and model learning. Suggested skills stay as candidates until a human approves, edits, rejects, or deletes them.

1. CheckRun verification against jobs and courses with thin skill evidence.
2. ReviewInspect AI suggestions, source, type, and supporting evidence.
3. ApproveApproved rows become reviewed skills and enter extracted skill lists.
4. LearnReviewed skills and aliases feed the next analysis run.
